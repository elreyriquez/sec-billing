Included at this tier:

  • Up to 50 active clients
  • Up to 100 invoices per calendar month
  • CSV exports (invoices, payments, clients — depth suited to Solo)
  • Manual payment recording, PDFs, core reporting suitable for a single operator

Not included on Solo (upgrade for these):

  • Client portal, recurring invoices, email automation
  • Advanced reports tier, multi-seat collaboration, higher volume caps
