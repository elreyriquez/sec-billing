Specifications

✓Multi-tenant workspaces with strict organization isolation
✓Clients and invoices with line items, tax, and manual payment recording (including partial payments)
✓Due dates with search, filters, and sorting by client, due date, amount, and invoice number
✓Invoice workflow: draft and ready states, collaborator edits, request send, and void approvals with admin override where configured
✓Statuses for draft, ready, sent, overdue, paid, and void; balance tracking after payments
✓Branded invoice PDFs and optional organization logo on PDFs and client portal
✓Client portal via secure links: view invoice, download PDF, optional payment-initiated note
✓Time tracking for admins and bookkeepers: one running timer per user (server-side), manual duration entry, edit past entries, billable flag, and optional rate overrides
✓Default hourly rates at organization, user, and client level for estimated time value in reports (operational insight, not payroll or GAAP)
✓Time reports: hours by month and by client; admins can compare bookkeepers and view org-wide time (viewers have no time access)
✓Performance reports with charts; export period reports as PDF with chart images
✓CSV exports for accountants (invoices, payments, clients, and line items; depth varies by plan)
✓Recurring invoices / retainers with scheduled draft generation
✓Email templates and optional reminder automation with monthly send caps per tier
✓Roles: admin, bookkeeper, and viewer — Studio and Agency let you assign every seat to admin or bookkeeper if needed (viewer when you want read-only)
✓Password reset, email job history with retry for failed sends, and audit logging for operations
